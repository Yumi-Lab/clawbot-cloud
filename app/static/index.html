FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is my data really local?

Yes. OpenJarvis runs entirely on the device plugged into your home. Your files and conversations never leave your local network. The cloud is only used for AI model routing and account management — never your personal data.

What happens when I hit my quota?

We never cut you off. Like a mobile plan, once you exceed the quota, speed decreases but the service continues. You can upgrade anytime with prorated billing.

Does it work without internet?

Your data, files, and local tools remain accessible on the device at all times. AI features require an internet connection since AI models run in the cloud — but your private data stays on your device regardless.

Can I have multiple devices?

Yes. One subscription, as many devices as you want. Kids use the parent device in Safe mode.

What’s the difference with Alexa or Siri?

Alexa and Siri answer questions. OpenJarvis acts: it runs code, manages your files, controls your tools. It’s a system employee, not a voice chatbot.

What are AI employees?

AI employees are specialized agents you hire for specific roles — accountant, researcher, coach, personal assistant. Each has its own identity, memory, and skills. They work together as a team on your device.

Who makes OpenJarvis?

OpenJarvis is designed by Yumi Lab, specialists in embedded electronics and 3D printing. The hardware uses the Smart Pi One, our own board.
